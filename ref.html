Recommended Books

Patanjali Yoga Sutras: The Heart of Yoga Recommended

by Gurudev Sri Sri Ravi Shankarji

This is a must-have book for those seeking to understand Patanjali's Yoga Sutras. Gurudev's explanations are lucid and accurate without sacrificing any details. The numerous anecdotes included make it engaging and enjoyable to read. It also features Q&A sections addressing frequently asked questions. The sutra translations on this site are largely sourced from this book. However, literal translations are simply not enough to grasp the true depth and nuances of the sutras, so purchasing the book is highly recommended.
