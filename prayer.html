Yoga Sutra Notes

Prayer

योगेन चित्तस्य पदेन वाचां
मलं शरीरस्य च वैद्यकेन ।
योऽपाकरोत् तं प्रवरं मुनीनां
पतञ्जलिं प्राञ्जलिरानतोऽस्मि ॥
yogena cittasya padena vācāṃ
malaṃ śarīrasya ca vaidyakena
yo'pākarot taṃ pravaraṃ munīnāṃ
patañjaliṃ prāñjalirānato'smi
I offer salutation with folded hands
to the noblest in the lineage of sages,
Maharṣi Patanjali,
who removed impurity
from the mind through yoga,
from speech through grammar,
and from the body through medicine.
